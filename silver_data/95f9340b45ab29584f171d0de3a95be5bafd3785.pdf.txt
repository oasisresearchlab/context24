Chemistry of Heterocyclic Compounds , Vol . 49 , No . 8 , November , 2013 ( Russian Original Vol . 49 , No . 8 , August , 2013 ) STEPWISE SYNTHESIS OF 2 - AMINO - 3 - VINYLINDOLIZINES USING A MIXTURE OF N - ALLYL - 2 - HALOPYRIDINIUM HALIDES AND CH ACID DERIVED FROM ACETONITRILE N . M . Tverdokhleb 1 and G . E . Khoroshilov 1 * N - Allyl - 2 - bromo - and N - allyl - 2 - chloropyridinium halides react with malonodinitrile and thiazolyl derivatives of acetonitrile in the presence of a twofold excess of triethylamine to provide nucleophilic substitution products , which undergo Thorpe cyclization smoothly upon treatment with KOH to give the corresponding 2 - amino - 3 - vinylindolizines . Keywords : N - allylpyridinium salts , 2 - amino - 3 - vinylindolizines , indolizine . It has been established that Kröhnke salts [ 1 ] react with CH acids ( derived from acetonitrile ) in the presence of a twofold excess of base to give 2 - amino - 3 - aroylindolizines 2 [ 2 , 3 ] . There is a considerable interest in aroylindolizines 2 as building blocks for obtaining condensed heterocyclic systems with potential pharmacological activity [ 4 - 6 ] : N Cl Ar O N NH 2 Z O Ar CN Z 2 Et 3 N + 1 2 + Br – Z = CN , CO 2 Et , CONHAr , 2 - ( 4 - aryl ) phenylthiazolyl , 2 - benzimidazolyl This method may be used to obtain indolizines 2 with electron - withdrawing substituents at position 3 . Studying the biological properties of such derivatives requires the preparation of indolizines with other substituents . We decided to introduce a vinyl group at position 3 , which may undergo subsequent functionalization . The previously unreported mixture of N - allyl - 2 - chloro - ( 3a ) and N - allyl - 2 - bromopyridinium salts ( 3b ) was used as an alternative to Kröhnke salts as a source of the pyridine fragment and vinyl group . The 1 H NMR spectra indicate that halides 3a , b are formed in approximately equal amounts . The signals for the pyridine ring cation protons H - 6 and H - 5 overlap and appear as a multiplet ( overlap of two doublets ) and triplet at 9 . 58 - 9 . 59 and 8 . 23 ppm , respectively . The signal for the H - 5 proton has a coupling constant J = 6 . 8 Hz , which is typical for the pyridine ring of such systems [ 7 ] . However , doubling of the signals is seen for the H - 3 ( doublets ) and H - 4 ( triplets ) protons , probably due to a different shielding effect of the halogens ( Fig . 1 ) . Fig . 1 . In conclusion , a simple method for the synthesis of 2 - amino - 3 - vinylindolizines has been developed with broad possibilities for functionalization of the pyrrole fragment of the indolizine molecule . EXPERIMENTAL The IR spectra were recorded on a Perkin - Elmer Spectrum One spectrometer for KBr pellets . The 1 H NMR spectra were recorded for compound 8d on a Varian VRX - 200 spectrometer ( 200 MHz ) and for the other compounds on a Bruker Avance II - 400 spectrometer ( 400 MHz ) . The 13 C NMR spectra were recorded on a Bruker Avance II - 400 spectrometer ( 100 MHz ) . The solvent for all the NMR spectra was DMSO - d 6 , while TMS served as internal standard . The mass spectra were recorded on an MKh - 1321 mass spectrometer with direct inlet of the sample into the ion source . The ionizing voltage was 70 eV . The elemental analysis was carried out on a Eurovector EA - 3000 instrument . The melting points were determined on a Kofler hot bench . The course of the reactions and purity of the products were monitored by thin - layer chromatography on Silufol UV - 254 plates using 3 : 5 acetone  hexane as the eluent and iodine vapor or UV light for visualization .
