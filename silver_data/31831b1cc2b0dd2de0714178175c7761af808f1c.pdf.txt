Marshall Supplemental Experimental Procedures Cell tracking in IBIDI chambers Real - time videomicroscopic tracking of cell migration within collagen matrices was performed as described ( Friedl et al . , 2004 ) , with the exception that the gel was polymerized in a commercially available vessel ( uncoated - Slide I flow chamber ; IBIDI Diagnostics , Munich , Germany ) equivalent in depth to the self - constructed chamber described previously ( Friedl et al . , 2004 ) Adhesion assays Cells were non - ezymatically removed ( using Gibco - BRL # 13150 - 016 ) before replating in 12 well plates that were coated with 1 . 8mg / ml Collagen type 1 in DMEM + 1 % FCS . After 2h hours non - adherent cells were removed by tipping the media out and washing twice with PBS . Adherent cells were then fixed using 4 % PFA and the number of cells in three 20x fields was counted . Supplemental References Friedl , P . & Brocker , E . B .
