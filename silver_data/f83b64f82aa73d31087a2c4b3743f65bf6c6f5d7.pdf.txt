Eur J Pediatr 181 : 3995 – 4009 Abstract SARS - CoV - 2 associated disease burden in children and adolescents Since early 2020 , the role of children was of key importance in the coronavirus disease 2019 ( COVID - 19 ) pandemic , especially in the public debate . This article provides a retrospective summary of the disease burden ofchildren due to infections with severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 ( SARS - CoV - 2 ) 3 years after the beginning of the pandemic . First , the evolution of the virus and the eﬀects on the manifestation and epidemiology of SARS - CoV - 2 infections in childhood are discussed . In this context , an overview is then given of the three severe manifestations of SARS - CoV - 2 infections in children , severe COVID - 19 , pediatric inﬂammatory multisystem syndrome ( PIMS ) and post - COVID syndrome . Furthermore , the diﬃculties in quantifying the real disease burden due to SARS - CoV - 2 infections in children are discussed .
