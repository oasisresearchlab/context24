Research Motivation & Aim Research Aim Broad Objective • Increase understanding of Harassment and Abused videos on YouTube . • Investigate effective solution to combat the Cyber Harassment problem on YouTube . Specific Objective • Characterization study on real world dataset . • Examine one class classifier for the task of recognizing Privacy Invading Harassment and Misdemeanour videos . 12 . . . Related work & Research Contributions Related Work 13 Research Study Research Aim Chaudhary et . al ; 2013 A one class classifier approach for detecting video response spam ( Promotional , Botnet and offensive videos ) on YouTube . Dadvar et . al ; 2012 Investigate the user information ( gender and age ) to detect cyber bullying in MySpace . Deniz O . et . al ; 2012 Propose a method to detect fighting in videos using by analysing acceleration patterns . Theodoros et . al ; 2010 Describes a multi - model approach to identify violence in videos by using audio , visual and textual features . Lee et . al ; 2009 Describes a multimedia and contextual mining based approach to detect offensive content in videos . Related work & Research Contributions Research Contributions • First step in the direction of applying a one - class classifier based approach for detecting Privacy Invading Harassment and Misdemeanor using contextual features of videos on YouTube . • Empirical analysis and identification of discriminatory features on YouTube metadata for the task of harassment detection . 14 . . .
