Family history : Patients who had at least one first or second degree relative with PD were classified as familial . All others were classified as non - familial ( sporadic ) . MAF of the risk allele and OR were significantly higher in sporadic PD than familial PD ( P = 0 . 01 for MAF , P = 0 . 02 for OR ) , however , the differences were not significant after adjusting for number of strata tests performed . Age at onset : Late - onset defined as age > 50 years , early onset as ≤ 50 years . Smoker : > 100 cigarettes in lifetime .
